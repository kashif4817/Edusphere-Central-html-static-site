Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?

Our subscriptions are monthly and renew automatically. You can cancel anytime from your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access to your plan until the end of the current billing cycle.

Are notes updated regularly?

Absolutely! We continuously update our notes to reflect the latest curriculum changes and add new content based on student feedback and exam patterns.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods including PayPal, Stripe, and local payment options.

Is there a student discount available?

Yes! Contact us with your student ID, and we'll provide you with a special discount code for any of our paid plans.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you choose the right plan.
